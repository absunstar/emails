What is temp mail?

Temp mail, also called temporary email or disposable email, is an inbox you use for a limited task instead of exposing your main address. It helps reduce inbox clutter and lowers the chance that your personal email is added to lists you did not choose.

Best uses for a temporary email address

  • Testing websites, apps, forms, and sign-up flows.
  • Receiving confirmation messages for short-term accounts.
  • Keeping promotions and trial emails outside your main inbox.
  • Managing privacy-focused browsing and research sessions.

Privacy note

Temporary email is useful for low-risk, short-term messages. Do not use disposable inboxes for banking, private documents, password recovery, or sensitive personal data.
