Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VIP Temp Mails (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) handles user privacy when you use our mobile application. VIP Temp Mails provides temporary email services that allow users to access disposable email addresses and receive messages through the App.

Summary: We do not sell your personal data. According to our current app data practices, we do not collect personal data from users and we do not share user data with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

VIP Temp Mails does not require users to create a personal account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, phone number, address, personal email address, contacts, photos, or precise location.

The App may display or process temporary email addresses and received email messages as part of its core functionality. These emails are provided so users can access temporary inboxes inside the App.

2. How We Use Information

The App is designed to provide temporary email functionality. Any temporary email content available through the App is used only to:

  • Display temporary email addresses.
  • Allow users to access inbox messages.
  • Provide the basic functionality of the App.
  • Maintain and improve app stability and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user personal data. We do not share user data with third parties for advertising, tracking, or marketing purposes.

4. Temporary Email Content

Temporary email services may receive messages from third-party websites or services when you use a temporary email address. You should not use temporary email addresses to receive sensitive information, financial information, private credentials, medical information, legal documents, or any information that requires long-term privacy or security.

You are responsible for how you use temporary email addresses and for any information sent to those addresses.

5. Children’s Privacy

VIP Temp Mails is not directed to children for the purpose of collecting personal information. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the App and its services. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. Temporary email should not be used for highly sensitive or confidential communications.

7. Third-Party Services

The App may interact with third-party websites or services when users receive emails from external senders. These third-party services are not controlled by us and are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

8. Data Retention

VIP Temp Mails is designed to provide access to temporary email functionality. Email availability may depend on the service infrastructure and app functionality. We recommend that users do not rely on temporary email addresses for permanent storage of important messages.

9. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have privacy rights such as requesting access, correction, or deletion of personal data. Since the App does not require personal account registration and does not intentionally collect personal data, such requests may be limited to information you voluntarily provide when contacting support.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
